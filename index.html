The story is set in a world where portals called "Gates" connect Earth to dungeons filled with monsters.
Some humans, known as "Hunters," possess supernatural powers that allow them to fight these monsters.
Among the Hunters, there are rankings from E to S class, with S being the most powerful.


The protagonist, Sung Jin-Woo, is initially the weakest E-rank Hunter, often referred to as "the world's weakest."
Despite his low rank, Jin-Woo continues to work as a Hunter to pay for his mother's hospital bills.

One day, while on a dungeon raid, Jin-Woo and his party encounter a double dungeon filled with deadly traps
and powerful enemies. Many of his comrades die, and Jin-Woo barely survives.

After sacrificing himself to save his team, Jin-Woo wakes up in a hospital and discovers he has gained a mysterious
ability called the System. This ability allows him to "level up" and grow stronger like a character in a video game.
